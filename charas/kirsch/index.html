Personal Data 1

様々な製菓道具に変化させることができる大きな泡だて器型のパラレルウェポンを携えたパティシエヒーローに変身する。その泡だて器は大気に渦を巻き起こす能力を有し、師匠から受け継いだ妙技「回転製菓術」によって多種多様な菓子を味方に振り撒く。パラレルウェポンの能力を駆使して作り出した彼女の菓子には、摂取したヒーローとパラレルウェポン双方の疲弊を回復させる効果を持つ。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

She transforms into a pâtissier hero with a large whisk-shaped parallel weapon that can be transformed into various confectionery tools. The whisk has the ability to create vortices in the atmosphere, and with the “spinning confectionery technique” that she inherited from her master, she is able to sprinkle a wide variety of confectionery for her ally. Her confections, created using her parallel weapon abilities, have the effect of restoring the exhaustion of both the heroes who ingest it and their parallel weapon.

Personal Data 2

様々な点で破天荒な彼女だが、その願いも「パティシエ生命に賭けて幻のスイーツを再現すること」という、これまた破天荒なもの。かつて師匠が銀河の果てで出会い、九死に一生の経験を経てレシピに起こしたという「幻のスイーツ」を自身の手で再現できる日が来るまで、彼女は戦うパティシエであり続ける事だろう。ちなみに、作ったお菓子を「美味しい」と言ってもらえることが、一番嬉しいらしい。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

She is unconventional in many respects, but her wish is also unconventional: to risk her life as a pâtissier to recreate the ultimate confection. She will continue to be a fighting pâtissier until the day comes when she can recreate the “Legendary Sweets” that her master once met at the edge of the galaxy and wrote in his recipe after a near-death experience. By the way, what makes her happiest is when people say that her sweets are delicious.

Side Story

とある街の洋菓子店でパティシエとして働く、宇宙人の少女。「嵐を呼ぶパティシエ」の異名を持ち、菓子作りとはおよそ縁遠いスポ根的な熱血精神で以て日々菓子作りに精進している。かつて究極の菓子作りの修行の中でカイブツに食われ消えていった師匠の意志を受け継ぎ、破天荒な製法で菓子を作り上げる。菓子作りに関すること以外は無関心であり、なかなかに頓珍漢な言動が目立つ。

Side Story (Translated)

An alien girl who works as a pâtissier at a pastry shop in a certain town.

Known as “The Pâtissier Who Calls Upon the Storm”, she makes sweets holding a hot blooded fighting spirit far unsuited for the role every day.

She took over the wishes of her teacher, who was eaten by a kaibutsu during his training to make the ultimate confection, using an unprecedented manufacturing method.

Claiming to be indifferent about anything unrelated to confectionery, both her words and her deeds are conspicuous and tend to contradict each other.
