Personal Data 1

宇宙中の先端技術を見て回る事を夢見た少女が、大人になってから当時の夢を思い出し、再びその夢を胸に抱くことに決心した結果、様々なメカを解析できる性能を持った工具を武器にして戦うヒーローが誕生した。彼女の担ぐ巨大な工具は、目の前に存在する対象が持つ武器の性能や構造を的確に解析し、様々な工具の形態に変化し、対象を分解する事が出来る一種のスーパーコンピューターでもある。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

A young girl who dreamed of seeing the most advanced technology in the universe remembers her dreams when she was an adult. She decides to hold that dream in her heart again, and the result is a hero who fights with a tool that has the ability to analyze a variety of mechanisms. The huge tool she carries accurately analyzes the performance and structure of any object that she sees. It is also a kind of supercomputer that can change into various forms of tools and disassemble objects.

Personal Data 2

「男」はこうあれ、「女」はこうあれ…彼女を取り巻く環境は、いつだってそのような言葉に満ちていた。周囲の人々曰く「男」的である趣味や性格を持つ彼女は、常に浮いた存在で、「女」らしくすることを望まれ続けてきた。彼女の持つ夢に対しても同じようなことを言及され、「女」らしい夢を持つことを切望された。そんな彼女が夢を一度捨てるまで、そう長い時間はかからなかったという。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

A “man” should be this way, a “woman” should be that way… The environment around her was always filled with such words. With hobbies and personalities that are “masculine” according to those around her, she has always feel out of place and wanted to be a “woman” in her own right. The same thing was mentioned about her dream, and she longed to have a “womanly” dream. It didn’t take long for her to abandon her dream once.

Side Story

地球有数のシェアを誇る宇宙船製造企業・ラムジェット社に所属する整備士で、パラレルフライト社と提携契約を結んでいる専属メカニック兼パイロット。米国シアトル出身の地球人で、フルネームはクローネ・アップルフィールド。気も腕っぷしも強く、「女だから」とナメられることを非常に嫌う。先端技術に興味深々なギークで、珍しいメカやロボットを見ると、解体して構造を解析したがる悪癖がある。

Side Story (Translated)

A mechanic affiliated with Ramjet: a spacecraft manufacturing company with the largest market share on Earth.

She is an in-house mechanic and spaceship pilot who has a partnership agreement with the company where the Protagonist works at. An Earthling from Seattle, USA. Her full name is Crowne Applefield.

Her arms are as strong as her spirit, and she abhors being treated with contempt “because she’s a woman”.

Since she’s a geek deeply interested in high tech, when she sees rare mechas or robots she has a bad habit of wanting to disassemble them and analyze their structure.
