Main Quest Chapter 2
Duration

The main quests of chapter 2 has been released. Completion of chapter 2 main quests will give you レジェンドメダル x 1 and エーテル晶石 x 10.

More episodes will be released at the following schedule (subject to changes).

Scheduled release date Episodes
2020/11/18 1~3
2020/11/25 4~6
2020/12/2 7~9

In commemoration of the new chapter release, a limited time gacha summon is also released which include Gammei (Hero and Sidekick), Suhail (Hero and Sidekick) and Mokdai (Hero and Sidekick).
